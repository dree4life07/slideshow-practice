Claudia R. Crenshaw

PhD, APRN

Dr. Crenshaw is pleased to announce the opening of her psychotherapy practice in Morgan County after working in Decatur for over 30 years.

She believes everyone has an intrinsic nature and the internal resources to heal. Specializing in trauma, loss, anxiety, depression, addiction, communication and relationships, Dr. Crenshaw has a unique ability to find the root issue and help her clients get back on track as an advanced psychiatric clinical nurse specialist and licensed professional counselor.

With three adult daughters, a loving partner, four young grandsons and two lively grand-puppies, she enjoys all outdoor activities, her garden, the peacefulness of Lake Oconee and the opportunity to serve her community.
